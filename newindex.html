FAQ

Common questions.

Does cpulze work for small properties with only a handful of reviews?
Yes — in fact, smaller properties often see the most dramatic results. With fewer reviews in circulation, a single negative quote carries disproportionate weight in AI responses. cpulze is designed for independent and boutique hotels of any size, from 8-room guesthouses to 80-room independent properties.
How long does it typically take for the AI narrative to shift after I log a fix?
Most properties see measurable shifts within 4–10 weeks of logging a fix. Simpler issues with a single dominant source resolve faster; complex narratives spanning multiple review platforms can take 12–16 weeks. We monitor continuously and show you the percentage shift as it happens — you're never left guessing.
Which AI engines do you scan?
We scan Perplexity, ChatGPT (GPT-4), and Google Gemini — the three platforms that now answer the majority of AI-assisted hotel searches. Each engine weights sources differently, so we report findings separately per engine so you can see exactly where the problem is strongest.
Do I need any technical knowledge to use cpulze?
None at all. You run the scan, read the plain-language report, fix the issue your way (that part is operational, not technical), and log a one-paragraph summary in PropertyVoice. Everything else is handled by cpulze. If you can write a TripAdvisor response, you can use cpulze.
What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?
Your scan history and PropertyVoice entries remain accessible for 90 days after cancellation so you can export what you need. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted. We never share or sell your property data. You can review our full Privacy Policy at any time.
How is this different from managing my reviews on TripAdvisor or Google?
Review management improves your star rating and helps future guests who read reviews directly. cpulze addresses a separate and newer problem: what AI engines say about you in response to direct questions. A guest asking ChatGPT "are rooms quiet at X hotel?" will never visit your TripAdvisor page — they get an AI-generated answer. cpulze targets that exact moment.
